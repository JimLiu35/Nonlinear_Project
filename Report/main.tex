\section{Control Derivation}
Given the dynamics of a real-wheel-drive car-like model,
\begin{flalign}
  \dot{x} & = vcos(\theta)\nonumber\\
  \dot{y} & = vsin(\theta)\nonumber\\
  \dot{\theta} & = \frac{vtan(\phi)}{l}\\
  \dot{v} & = u_1\nonumber\\
  \dot{\phi} & = u_2\nonumber
\end{flalign}
